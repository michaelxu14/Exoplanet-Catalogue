ABOUT THIS PROJECT

Welcome to the Exoplanet Habitability Catalogue (EHC), a project dedicated to cataloguing our best chances of finding habitable worlds beyond our solar system. The EHC is a comprehensive resource for storing exoplanet data, and presents the best opportunities for finding a new home in the stars. This project is designed to be a user-friendly interface for exploring exoplanet data; in a world where exoplanet research is sensationalized and This catalogue explores the potential for habitability among discovered exoplanets. Using real astronomical datasets, we visualize Earth Similarity Index values and highlight planets most likely to support life as we know it.

Our goal is to bring together data and visualization to inspire curiosity about our universe and the search for habitable worlds.

The EHC is built using Django. We hope you find this project informative and inspiring as we continue to explore the cosmos.
